Processed Plays

Path: s3://{bucket}/processed/season=YYYY/week=W/*.parquet
Generated by: src/processing/json_to_parquet.py
Format: Snappy compressed Parquet (partitioned by season and week)

Overview: This is the base play by play table. It takes the raw JSON from the CollegeFootballData API, checks it against the schema, and writes it out. Note: Special teams plays (punts, kickoffs, PATs) are included in this table, but are filtered out later during the Stage 3 aggregations.

Fields:

gameId (Long, Not Nullable): Game ID pulled straight from the API.
driveId (String, Nullable): ID for the specific drive.
id (String, Not Nullable): Unique ID for the play.
driveNumber (Integer, Nullable): Which drive of the game this is.
playNumber (Integer, Nullable): Which play of the drive this is.
offense (String, Nullable): Team with the ball.
offenseConference (String, Nullable): Offensive team's conference.
offenseScore (Integer, Nullable): Offense's score when the ball was snapped.
defense (String, Nullable): Team on defense.
defenseConference (String, Nullable): Defensive team's conference.
defenseScore (Integer, Nullable): Defense's score when the ball was snapped.
home (String, Nullable): Home team name.
away (String, Nullable): Away team name.
period (Integer, Nullable): Quarter (1–4). Anything >4 means its OT.
clock.minutes (Integer, Nullable): Minutes left on the clock.
clock.seconds (Integer, Nullable): Seconds left on the clock.
offenseTimeouts (Integer, Nullable): Timeouts the offense has left.
defenseTimeouts (Integer, Nullable): Timeouts the defense has left.
yardline (Integer, Nullable): Distance from the offense's own goal line (0 to 100).
yardsToGoal (Integer, Nullable): Distance to the end zone.
down (Integer, Nullable): Current down (1–4). This is null for special teams.
distance (Integer, Nullable): Yards to the sticks. Also null for special teams.
yardsGained (Integer, Nullable): Net yardage on the play (negatives mean a loss).
scoring (Boolean, Nullable): True if the play resulted in points.
playType (String, Nullable): Play category (Rush, Pass, Punt, Kickoff, etc.).
playText (String, Nullable): The actual play by play text description.
ppa (Double, Nullable): Predicted Points Added. Null if it's not a scrimmage play.
wallclock (Timestamp, Nullable): Real world time the play happened.
season (Integer, Partition Key): The season year (e.g., 2025).
week (Integer, Partition Key): Week of the season (1–15).

Team Game Stats

Path: s3://{bucket}/stats/team_game_stats.parquet
Generated by: src/processing/aggregate_stats.py
Format: Snappy compressed Parquet

Overview: This table holds the game level offensive stats for each team, generated by the Stage 3 MapReduce job. This table only looks at scrimmage plays. Any play without a valid down and distance is dropped before aggregation. Each game generates two rows here (one for each team's offensive performance).

Fields:

gameId (Long, Not Nullable): The game this row belongs to.
season (Integer, Not Nullable): Season year.
week (Integer, Not Nullable): Week number.
team (String, Not Nullable): The team playing offense.
opponent (String, Not Nullable): The team playing defense.
is_home (Boolean, Not Nullable): True if the team was playing at home.
plays (Long, Not Nullable): Total number of scrimmage plays run (special teams excluded).
total_yards (Long, Not Nullable): Sum of yardsGained across all scrimmage plays.
yards_per_play (Double, Not Nullable): total_yards / plays (rounded to 3 decimals).
success_rate (Double, Not Nullable): % of plays that hit the success thresholds: 1st down (>= 50% of distance), 2nd down (>= 70%), 3rd/4th down (100%).
third_down_attempts (Long, Not Nullable): Total 3rd downs faced.
third_down_conv (Long, Not Nullable): Total 3rd downs converted.
third_down_pct (Double, Nullable): third_down_conv / third_down_attempts (rounded to 3 decimals). Null if they somehow didn't face a single 3rd down.
big_plays (Long, Not Nullable): Any play that gained 20+ yards.
big_play_pct (Double, Not Nullable): big_plays / plays (rounded to 3 decimals).
scoring_plays (Long, Not Nullable): Total plays where scoring was true.
avg_ppa (Double, Nullable): Average PPA across all scrimmage plays (rounded to 4 decimals). Null if the API didn't provide PPA data.
